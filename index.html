All your data is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. Your habits and progress never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Currently, data is stored per device. We're working on cloud sync in a future update that will let you access your data across devices.

Missing a day won't delete your progress, but it will reset your streak. Don't worry - every day is a new opportunity to start fresh!
